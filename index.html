The next generation of media

My name is Kyle LeBlanc and I am a self taught developer currently living in Ottawa Ontario. I have been toying with the idea of becoming a developer for many years without a real commitment. I started college right out of high school for video game development but didn't complete it due to financial issues among others. To be completely honest, I wasn't ready right out of school I realize now.

Since that time, I have spent my time learning new skills that would allow me to enter a career in the technology sector. I have considered going back to college to receive a computer science degree, however I am an excellent self taught learner, and while I believe it would be a good investment. I believe that I can achieve my goals without it, by demonstrating my dedication, learning abilities to acquire additional skills or languages, and my passion for creating things that matter to peoples lives.
